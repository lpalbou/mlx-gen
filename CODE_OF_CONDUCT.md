# Code Of Conduct

MLX-Gen follows a simple standard for participation: be respectful, constructive, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Give technical feedback with enough context to be useful.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about bugs, risks, and tradeoffs.
- Respect maintainers' time and project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publicly sharing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers have redirected the conversation.
- Spam or automated low-quality contributions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when behavior is harmful to the project. Serious security or abuse concerns should be reported privately when possible.
